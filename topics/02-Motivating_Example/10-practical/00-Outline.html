Analysis of student marks using pandas

This week, please answer the following questions from the data:

  1. What is the average mark per module?
  2. What is the average mark per student?
  3. What are the top two modules per student?
  4. What are the top two students per module?
  5. On average, do students achieve higher marks in exams than in practical (i.e., non-exam) assessments?
